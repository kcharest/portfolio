I’m Kalyn Charest, a UI/UX Designer and Illustrator living in the Twin Cities.

I’ve been drawing since I could hold a pencil and it was only inevitable that I would end up in the creative career of design. I attended the Savannah College of Art and Design where I received a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in the field of Illustration. After working as a freelance illustrator for several years I made the choice to expand my design skills into the area of user interface and experience design.

Combining my fine art knowledge and design skills I strive to create the perfect balance between artful visuals and intuitive user centered design. I am currently pursuing a full time position in the feild of UI/UX. If you like what you see then let's get in touch.
